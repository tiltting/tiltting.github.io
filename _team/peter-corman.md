**Biography**  
Peter Corman is a Meteorology Major with a minor in Emergency Management. From Great Meadows, New Jersey, Peter has been in love with the weather since a very young age. Peter enjoys forecasting the most, as he's spent his last several years forecasting severe, tropical, and winter weather online. At Millersville, Peter has gotten himself involved with Campus Weather Service and WxChallenge, in addition to being a member of the Student Chapter.  Outside of Meteorology, Peter enjoys listening to music, playing video games, taking photos, and hanging out with his friends.
